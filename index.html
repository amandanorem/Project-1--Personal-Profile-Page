Amanda Norem, Web Designer.

Hello, from sunny Southern California.

Background

Amanda Norem is a southern belle from Louisiana, but moved out to Southern California with her husband and three kids nine years ago. She has been a stay at home mother for 16 years focusing all her attention on her family.

Having three teenagers that are getting ready to leave for college within the next four years, she wanted something to occupy the growing abundance available time. During a time her husband was deployed for six months, Amanda and her daughter started a blog. Blogging helped Amanda realized she had a passion for web development and design. Amanda decided to teach herself how to use HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Amanda has developed and designed many websites. She has designed logos, as well. Seeing her projects come to life gives her a sense of self worth and adds fuel to her desires to become a sucessful web designer.
